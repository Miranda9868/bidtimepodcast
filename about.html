Hello!

Welcom to Bid Time Podcast this is a place where I assist cattlemen with organizing their sale offering and give them the platform to tell the world their story and what they believe makes their cattle elite.

This platform allows breeders to connect with their customers through social media sound bytes and podcasting. This allows us at Bid Time to share your story with the cattle industry. Bid Time is a subsidary of Sire Buyer and utilizes Sire Buyer Live to offer sales for breeders online.

I am your host, Ethan Johnson. I am a young cattle enthusiast from Paris, Kentucky. I currently live in Falls City, Nebraska with my girlfriend, Miranda, where we help manage a large angus operation.

It is my mission to use this platform to revolutionize online sales and share the story of cattlemen and women across the country. Through sharing your story and creating awareness for your sale offering, Bid Time aims to give cattlemen a more personalized approach to online sales and assist them in reaching their target market.
