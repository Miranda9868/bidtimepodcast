Welcome to Bid Time Podcast!

Bid Time Podcast is your way to tell the world about your livestock or products sold on auctions.sirebuyer.com. Bid Time's mission is to use this platform to revolutionize online sales and share the story of cattlemen and women across the country. Through sharing your story and creating awareness for your sale offering, Bid Time aims to give cattlemen a more personalized approach to online sales and assist them in reaching their target market.
